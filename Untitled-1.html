Who are chemical engineers?

Chemical Engineers convert raw materials into functional products using some optimized products. Chemical engineers are the ones who focus on improving current industrial processes and optimizing them to better extent.They also are the ones who strive hard for keeping processes environmental friendly.

For example, we find optimal ways of producing lithium ion batteies and better ways to dispose them.

What do chemical engineers study?

They study Bachelors degree for 4-5 years and then advanced degree which is Masters Degree for 1-2 years.

Other engineering fields which a chemical engineer can look into

  • Environmental engineering
  • Nuclear engineering
  • Biochemical engineering
  • Petroleum engineering
